About Us

Welcome to MediSched, your premier online platform for scheduling medical, dental, and vision appointments. At MediSched, we believe in simplifying healthcare management by connecting patients and providers seamlessly through our user-friendly portal. Our mission is to enhance accessibility and streamline the scheduling process to ensure a smooth and efficient experience for all.

MediSched was created by four ambitious students; Nichole R., Ashley W., Louisa K., and Yvecca M. who participated in the N'power Path2Tech program. Their shared passion for technology and improving healthcare access led to the development of this innovative platform.
